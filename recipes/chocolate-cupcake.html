Chocolate Cupcake

A sweet chocolate cupcake to soothe your chocolate cravings

Chocolates are sweet, cupcakes are creamy, chocolate cupcakes gives you the best of both.

Here's how to make one yourself :).

Ingredients

  • A cup of milk, granulated sugar, vegetable oil, all-purpose-floor and hot water
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp baking soda and baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C) and line muffin tin with cupcake liners
  2. In large bowel, whisk together sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking (soda and powder), and salt
  3. Stir in milk and vegetable oil, until combined
  4. Stir in egg and vanilla extract
  5. Add hot water and stir until mixture is evenly combined
  6. Fill prepared muffin tin cavities just 2/3 of the way full
  7. Bake muffin tin for 18-25 minutes
  8. Allow cupcakes to cool completely before frosting

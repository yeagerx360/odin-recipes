Chocolate Brownies

Chocolate and brownies are brown and sweet

Chocolate brownies for your cravings, remember it's only for you.

Here's how to make one yourself :).

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • canola oil
  • vanila extract
  • water

Instructions

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients in seperate bowls
  2. Pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
  3. Bake for 40-45 minutes at about 325F
  4. Slice and serve

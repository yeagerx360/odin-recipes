Milk Sandwich

Bread and milk sandwich for you only

An easy to make milk and bread sandwich, a simple appetizer for you and your friends.

Here's how to make one yourself :).

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • Sweetened condensed milk as needed
  • Butter as needed

Instructions

  1. Take bread slice, spread butter all over it, sandwich with the other bread
  2. Cook in butter till golden in both sides
  3. Serve
